Church · Internal Tool

Community Care App

A community care team was running their entire operation — recipients, agencies, social workers, locations, and deliveries — off a single spreadsheet. Duplicated rows, no backups, no automation. Hours lost every day just keeping the data intact.

I rebuilt it as a proper web app: clean relational data, automated delivery scheduling, onboarding forms for new agencies and social workers, and a stats dashboard showing deliveries, recipients, and agency reach at a glance. They also gained a group events system with check-in.

Hours saved from daily admin — redirected to reaching more people
Delivery process fully automated, response times significantly faster
Replaced a fragile single spreadsheet with a reliable, scalable system
Church · Internal Tool

Church Growth & Stats App

The leadership team had no reliable way to track the health of their congregation over time. Attendance data lived in Planning Center but there was no way to slice it by location, age group, or discipleship stage — let alone spot trends.

I built a stats dashboard integrated with Planning Center that gives leaders a live view of congregation health: attendance by location, age group breakdowns, and progress through key milestones like Gateway, baptisms, and courses. Graphs surface patterns and trends that were previously invisible.

Leadership can now see congregation health at a glance across all locations
Attendance trends and patterns visible for the first time
Integrates directly with Planning Center — no double entry
